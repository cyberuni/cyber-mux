`cyber-mux` is a CLI for cross-multiplexer pane control. Open, send, submit, read, focus, and close
panes — plus git worktrees and named templates — the same way whether you are running under **tmux**,
**herdr**, or **WezTerm** (alpha).

## One command surface

	<LinkCard title="open" href="/cyber-mux/cli/open/" description="Open a new pane, tab, or workspace and launch a command in it." />

## One contract, three multiplexers

The same verbs mean the same thing on every backend; where a multiplexer lacks a tier or feature,
cyber-mux maps onto the closest thing it has and reports honestly what it could not do.
